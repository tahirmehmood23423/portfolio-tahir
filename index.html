An end-to-end AI-powered job application assistant — designed, built, and deployed solo in 3 weeks. Automates the five most time-consuming parts of job hunting:

Module 1 — Résumé Parser: Uploads a PDF/DOCX résumé and returns fully structured JSON — personal info, skills, experience, education, certifications.
Module 2 — Job Matcher: Two-pass scoring using Gemini embeddings (semantic similarity) + LLM requirement extraction. Returns a 0–100 match score with per-requirement breakdown.
Module 3 — Résumé Tailorer: Source-bound LLM rewriting — reorders and rewrites existing content for a specific job. Never invents information. Includes ATS checker.
Module 4 — Cover Letter Generator: Two-pass pipeline: extract talking points → write 4-paragraph letter in chosen tone (Professional / Conversational / Confident / Enthusiastic).
Module 5 — Job Discovery: Scrapes LinkedIn and Remotive in parallel, auto-extracts keywords from résumé, scores and ranks jobs. Zero LLM cost — pure keyword matching.

LexAI is a two-sided digital marketplace and case management platform for Pakistan's legal ecosystem — connecting clients with verified lawyers, while giving lawyers powerful digital tools to manage their entire practice.

For Clients: Search lawyers by specialty, city, fee, and rating. Book video/phone/in-person consultations. Track case progress in real time. Upload and share legal documents. Real-time messaging via WebSocket.
For Lawyers: Professional dashboard with booking management. Case management system with hearing scheduler and email alerts. Secure document vault. Client communication tools.
AI Chatbot (Planned): End-to-end RAG system answering legal queries over a 26GB corpus of Pakistani law books in English and Urdu. Built with LangChain, ChromaDB, province-specific filtering, and confidence-scored responses.
